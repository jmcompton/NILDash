Section 01

Introduction

NILDash is a NIL (Name, Image, and Likeness) deal intelligence platform designed for sports agents and college athletes. It provides tools for managing NIL opportunities, tracking outreach to brand partners, automating deliverable schedules, and communicating directly with sponsors through connected email and calendar integrations.

NILDash is operated by Compton Group LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the NILDash platform at https://mynildash.com (the "Service").

By accessing or using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the Service.

Questions? Contact us at any time at jmcompton04@gmail.com. We respond to all privacy inquiries within 5 business days.
Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of the Service:

Account Information

  • Name and email address provided at registration
  • Password (stored as a one-way cryptographic hash — we never store your plaintext password)
  • Role (sports agent or athlete) and associated agent–athlete relationship
  • Profile details such as sport, school, and social media handles (athletes)

Google Account Data (when you connect Google)

  • Gmail address associated with the connected Google account
  • OAuth refresh token allowing NILDash to send email and create calendar events on your behalf
  • We request only the minimum necessary scopes: gmail.send and calendar.events

Email and Calendar Content

  • Subject lines, body text, and recipient addresses of emails composed and sent through NILDash
  • Deliverable titles, due dates, and notes synced to Google Calendar
  • Email send history stored in your NILDash account for tracking purposes

Usage Data

  • Pages and features accessed within the platform
  • Actions taken (e.g., deals created, emails sent, deliverables completed)
  • Timestamps and activity logs used to improve the Service and provide analytics to agents
Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide, maintain, and improve the NILDash Service:

  • Platform functionality — to authenticate users, power the dashboard, and enable core features such as pipeline management, AI analysis, and reporting
  • Gmail sending — when an athlete connects their Gmail account, we use their OAuth token solely to send emails they compose in the NILDash portal, directly from their own Gmail address
  • Google Calendar sync — when a user connects Google Calendar, we create and update calendar events corresponding to NIL deliverables they choose to sync
  • Communications — to send transactional notifications (e.g., athlete invite emails, delivery confirmations) via our email provider
  • Platform improvement — to understand how features are used so we can improve the product
  • Security — to detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, or security incidents
We do not sell your data. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or Google user data to third parties for advertising or any other commercial purpose.
Section 04

Google API Services

NILDash uses Google API Services, including the Gmail API and Google Calendar API, to enable athletes and agents to send emails and manage deliverable schedules directly from their connected Google accounts.

Compliance with Google Policies. Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • We use Google user data only to provide or improve the user-facing features of NILDash that are clearly described in this Privacy Policy
  • We do not use Google user data for advertising purposes
  • We do not allow humans to read your Google data except with your affirmative consent, for security purposes, or as required by law
  • We do not transfer Google user data to third parties except as necessary to provide the Service (see Section 7) or as required by law

Scopes we request. NILDash requests only the minimum scopes necessary:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send — to send emails on behalf of the athlete from their own Gmail address
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events — to create and update deliverable events in Google Calendar
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email — to confirm which Google account was authorized

Disconnecting Google. Athletes and agents can disconnect their Google account at any time from the NILDash dashboard. Upon disconnection, the stored OAuth refresh token is immediately deleted from our database. We will not use revoked credentials to send email or access your calendar.

NILDash's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
Section 05

Data Storage and Security

All NILDash data is stored on Railway, a U.S.-based cloud infrastructure provider, in a PostgreSQL database. Railway employs industry-standard physical and network security controls.

We take the following measures to protect your information:

  • Password hashing — passwords are stored using bcrypt, a one-way hashing algorithm. We cannot recover or view your plaintext password.
  • HTTPS — all data in transit between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS.
  • OAuth token storage — Google OAuth refresh tokens are stored in the database with access restricted to the NILDash application server only.
  • Authentication — user sessions are protected with signed JSON Web Tokens (JWTs).
  • Access controls — agent accounts can only access their own athletes' data; athlete accounts can only access their own records.

While we implement commercially reasonable security measures, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.
